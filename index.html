Mithu Name Meaning, Origin, and Usage The name Mithu is of Indian origin and is typically given to girls. It is associated with the Hindu religion and is often linked to the number 8 in numerology. The name Mithu means "sweet," "lovable," and "cute," and is a variant spelling of Mitu. It is a unisex name, although it is more commonly used for females. The name is pronounced as "MEE-thoo" and is well-regarded in popular culture, symbolizing cuteness and affection.
